Hello!
My name is Nadya Izotova, I am an illustrator currently based in Belgrade, Serbia.

I've been drawing my whole life. Although I graduated from university as an engineer, I never forgot my artistic passion. One day I rediscovered my love for illustration while reading books to my son. Now I've spent past years pursuing my dream of being children's book illustrator. I mainly work digitally using Procreate, taking inspiration from real life to create cute, funny and charming characters. I'm always looking for new opportunities to evolve as an illustrator.

For inquiries or to explore potential collaborations, please feel free to contact me!
